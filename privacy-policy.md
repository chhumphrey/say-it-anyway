Say It Anyway: Grief Journal respects your privacy.

This app is designed to function entirely offline. All journal entries, recipient information, and other content you create are stored locally on your device. The app does not collect, transmit, or share personal data with any servers or third parties.

The app does not require user accounts, does not use analytics or tracking technologies, and does not display third-party advertisements in this version.

Say It Anyway: Grief Journal may provide links to external support resources (such as crisis or support hotlines). When you follow these links, you leave the app and are subject to the privacy policies of those external services.

This app does not provide medical, mental health, or professional advice. It is intended solely as a personal journaling and reflection tool.
